Permissions

Write and read external storage permission

These permissions are needed for downloading files and playing them in the app.
The app only saves .m4a files (audio files) and only works with these. The app doesn't access any other files on the user's device.

Record audio permission

This permission is needed for audio visualization within the app, the only sound recorded is the one played by the app.
The app does not record any other audio, including phone calls or other personal audio.
